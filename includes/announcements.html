Collaboration: A focus on process, not just product
Wednesday, October 10, 10:30am-Noon
Many faculty want to include collaborative learning in their courses but encounter many questions along the way: How will I keep students “on task”? Does it matter how students are paired? In this session, CMU Prof. Carolyn Rose will join us in discussing various ways to promote more effective collaboration and, in turn, better learning (especially in STEM topics).
